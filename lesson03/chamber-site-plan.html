Overview

Site Name

Yuba City Chamber of Commerce. This name accurately describes the general purpose of this website including the city name and what it represents: their Chamber of Commerce.

Site Purpose

Yuba City is a tight-knit community and we help to provide the tools to bring it closer together through providing community event calendars, promoting businesses, and providing oppurtunitities to get involved.

Where can I promote my local business?
How can I get in contact with my local Chamber of Commerce?
What kind of community events are happening this month?
